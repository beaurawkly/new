Kinetic Type

During my high school years, I was fortunate enough to have an influential teacher who had a profound impact on my life. One of the ways in which he helped me was by introducing me to the story of Don Quixote, which I have since come to love. In fact, it has become one of my favorites. I chose a quote from the movie that embodies the message of Don Quixote and created a kinetic type motion piece using After Effects.
